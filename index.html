The King of Fruits

Mangoes are one of the most popular and beloved fruits worldwide, known for their sweet, tropical flavor and juicy texture. With over 500 varieties grown globally, these delicious fruits have been cultivated for thousands of years.

Why Mangoes Are Amazing

Nutritional Powerhouse

Mangoes are packed with vitamins A and C, potassium, fiber, and antioxidants that support immune health and vision.

Versatile Ingredient

From smoothies and salads to chutneys and desserts, mangoes can be incorporated into countless delicious recipes.

Year-round Availability

Different varieties of mangoes are harvested at different times throughout the year, making them available in many regions year-round.

Popular Mango Varieties

🥭

Alphonso

Known as the "King of Mangoes," this Indian variety is famous for its rich, creamy flavor and smooth texture.

🥭

Ataulfo

Also known as honey or champagne mango, this Mexican variety is sweet with a buttery texture and small pit.

🥭

Kent

A large, juicy variety with minimal fiber and a rich, sweet taste. Popular in South America and Florida.

🥭

Keitt

A late-season mango with greenish skin even when ripe, sweet flesh, and a small seed.

Delicious Mango Recipes

Mango Salsa

Combine diced mango with red onion, jalapeño, cilantro, lime juice, and a pinch of salt for a refreshing topping for fish tacos or grilled chicken.

Mango Smoothie

Blend ripe mango chunks with yogurt, a splash of orange juice, and honey for a nutritious breakfast or snack.

Mango Sticky Rice

This Thai dessert combines sweet glutinous rice with coconut milk and fresh mango slices for a tropical treat.
